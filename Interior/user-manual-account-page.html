ACCOUNT PAGE MANUAL

Now that you have been introduced to your dashboard, let's look at your Account. You can access your account information by clicking on Account on the menu bar on the left of the page under Dashboard.
There are several sections on the account page. Here, you can update your payment method or delete it and change your password. Clicking the change password button will send you an email with a link requiring you to create a new password.
The following section allows you to change or update your email address.
To change or update your email address, you simply edit the address in the email address field and click the Update Email button. You should see this popup.
When you click OK, you will be logged out of OTTA. Go to your email account that you updated to and click the link to log back into OTTA. Then click on the account link in the left menu.
Scroll down until you see the Address section. Please fill in the required fields and click the Update Address button.
You should see this popup. Click OK, and you are back on the account page.
Next is the No Thank You List. This is where you can list any people or organizations that you want to avoid working for or with. Enter the information and click Save Changes, or leave it blank and move on.
Next is the Birthday section. You can update your birthday if it was previously entered incorrectly. If you make a change here, you must click the Update Birthday button.
The last section is the Billing History.
Here, you can see your billing history and even click the View Invoice link if you would like to print a copy.

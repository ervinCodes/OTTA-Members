New Member Registration Manual

While on the OTTA website, click on the Members link in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
If this is your first time visiting this page, please click the"Register" button in the lower right of the Member Sign-In box.
Please complete the Registration Form. Be sure to read the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy (click the links), upon completion check the boxes confirming you are in agreement. Then, click the Register button.
Check your email and confirm your account.
You should find the Welcome to OneTeam email. Click on the "Confirm Email Address" link to be taken back to OTTA member Sign-In.
At Member Sign-In, enter the email and password you used during the registration process and click the "Login" button.
Welcome to your Member Dashboard!
You begin with entering your payment method for the OTTA admin fee; this is also used as a secondary means of verification.
Click the "Add Payment Method" button.
OTTA uses a third-party payment system called STRIPE
We do not have any access to, nor will we ever store your credit card information on our site or in our database.
Enter your card number, expiration, CVC number (aka: CVV), and Zip, then click the "Save" button.
Members will always have the abiility to update the payment method or delete it.
COMPLETE ACCOUNT SET UP
Beneath the "Add Payment Method" box, you will see the "Complete Resume" button. Click on that button and begin entering your employment data.
Click the "Complete Resume" button to proceed.

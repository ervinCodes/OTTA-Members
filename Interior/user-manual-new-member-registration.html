REGISTRATION PROCESS

On the OTTA website, navigate to the top of the page and click the 'Members' link in the navigation bar.
To create an account, please click the 'Register' button located in the lower right of the 'Member Sign-In' box.
To register for an account:
Complete the Registration Form: Fill out all the required fields on the Registration Form.
Your 'Birthday' information is optional, it allows us to celebrate your special day!
Once you've completed the form, click the 'Register' button to be taken to the page displaying User Agreements.
Review and Agree: Terms of Use & Privacy Policy
Before you proceed, please take a moment to scroll down and review both of our User Agreements. When you are ready, you can agree by checking the boxes below. Once both boxes are checked, you can click the 'Submit' button to finalize your registration.
  • Terms of Use: This document outlines the rules for using our service. By checking the box below, you acknowledge that you have scrolled down, reviewed and understand these terms.
  • Privacy Policy: This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.By checking the box below, you acknowledge that you have scrolled down, reviewed, and understand this policy.
Locate your Welcome Email: Check your inbox for an email titled “Welcome to the Team”.
Verify your Email Address: Please click on the 'Confirm Email Address' button, this will redirect you to the OTTA Member Sign-In page.
Once on the Member Sign-In page, enter the email address and password you created during the registration process.
Click the 'Login' button to access your OTTA account.
Welcome to your Member Dashboard!
Start by entering your payment information for the OTTA Admin Fee. This payment method also serves as a secondary verification step to confirm your identity.
Click the "Add Payment Method" button.
Secure Payment Processing with Stripe:
OTTA uses Stripe, a secure third-party payment system, to process your payment for the admin fee. This ensures your credit card information remains secure. We do not have access to, nor will we ever store your credit card information on our servers.
Please enter your card number, expiration date, CVC number (aka: CVV), and your billing zip code, then click the 'Save & Pay button.
Members will always have the abiility to update the payment method or delete it.
COMPLETE ACCOUNT SET UP
Please note the message: “Your membership is currently pending approval”.
This requires the following:
  • 1. Registration
  • 2. Add payment method, click 'Save and Pay'
  • 3. Complete Resume of Qualifications
Once all of the above are completed, the OTTA Admin is notified that a new membership is pending approval. The Admin will then review the application and approve or reject it. Membership will only be rejected if the information provided is incomplete or violates the OTTA Terms of Use. In such cases, the Admin will reach out to the member to try to resolve the issue.
Once you've entered your payment information, click the 'Complete Resume' button located below the 'Add Payment Method' section. This takes you to the 'Resume of Qualifications' area where you will begin entering your employment data.
